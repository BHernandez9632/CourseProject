What BlackClover is About

The BlackClover anime is about a kid names Asta who was born into a world of magic with no magic. He was laughed at by many but he would always keep his hopes up always physically training his body doing countless pushups, situps, and sprints. He did all this with the intention of one day becoming the wizard king. This was a dreamed many made fun of because of his zero magic, eventually when the time came to get tested he did not receive a regular magic book they varied from one clover to four clover covered book. The higher the better normally the four clover was for those intended to be great wizards. On that day Asta received a five clover book known to be the book with a demon dwelling in it which was correct. The moment he recieved that book he gained anti-magic which allowed him to nullify all magic with his new weapons. That was the starting point for his journey to become the wizard king.
