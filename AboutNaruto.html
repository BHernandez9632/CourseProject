What Naruto is About

The anime Naruto, and Naruto Shippuden are about a ninja called Naruto. He was born in the village hidden in the leaf, where he was hated by everyone because he housed this beast inside him known as the ninetailed fox. The ninetails was one of nine beast known to be as powerful as natural disasters. The story goes on to explain how Naruto struggles in his early life with no real guidance and like any little kid he did anything to get attention. He would eventually graduate from his class and become a starter ninja or Genin which is the lowest possible rank. The story goes on to show his growth he becomes stronger along the way and even loses his best friend Sasuke which become his motivation to become stronger in order to get him to turn back to the good side.
