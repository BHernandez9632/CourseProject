What OnePiece is About

The anime OnePiece, This anime revolves around the main character and his crew.The main characters names is monkey d luffy or simply luffy. Luffy at a young age decided he wanted to become the pirate king. This being his goal and dream he set out to become pirate king at the age of 16. He had no crew when he first set out but as the show went on he slowly gathered his crew. The show is mostly about pirates fighting each other and the navy to achieve their goals. There is a lot of great plot build and well made fight scenes that are extremely captivating. He has a special ability gained from a "devil fruit" these are fruits that are known to give powers to the person who eats it depending on the type of fruit eaten. He ate the gum gum fruit which gave him the ability to stretch his body like a rubberband. Like all other fruits the draw back to eating them is losing the ability to swim which means if he falls in water he sinks with no chance of swimming.
